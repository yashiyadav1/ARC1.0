ARC1.0 - Advanced Reading Companion

About

ARC1.0 enhances readability by selectively bolding parts of words on web pages. It bolds the first two letters of words with 4 or more letters, the first letter of 3-letter words, and leaves shorter words unchanged. This visual modification aims to improve reading speed and comprehension by creating anchor points for the reader's eyes.
